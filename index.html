Denver Sports

Denver plays host to many different sports teams.

Perhaps most notable is the football team, The Denver Broncos, that have won three Super Bowls in 1998, 1999, and 2016.

Denver is also the home to the Major League Baseball team that is part of the National League West, the Colorado Rockies.
Their home turf is the Coors Field.

Another famous team that resides in Denver is the Colorado Avalanches.

They play at the Ball Stadium which is more well known as the Pepsi Center.

Another team that calls Denver its home is the Denver Nuggets. They are a professional basketball team that play in the Ball Arena along with the Avalanches but they have never won an NBA Championship.
